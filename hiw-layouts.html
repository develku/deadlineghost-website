How It Works

Three steps. Then the ghost takes over.

No setup, no configuration. Add a deadline and the ghost appears instantly.

1

Add a deadline

Create a task from the menu bar. Pick a quick time or set any date. A ghost spawns instantly.

2

Watch it approach

The ghost tracks your cursor everywhere. As time runs out, the bar shrinks and colors shift.

3

Face the consequence

When time's up, a fullscreen overlay blocks everything until you mark the task complete.
