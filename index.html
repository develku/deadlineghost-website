How It Works

Three steps. Then the ghost takes over.

No setup, no configuration. Add a deadline and the ghost appears instantly.

1

Add a deadline

Create a task from the menu bar. Pick a quick time (5m, 15m, 1h) or set any date. A ghost spawns and starts following your cursor.

2

Watch it approach

The ghost tracks your cursor everywhere. As time runs out, the bar shrinks, colors shift, and animations intensify.

3

Feel the pressure

As the deadline closes in, the ghost turns red, shakes violently, and the bar disappears. Combined with white noise, you feel the urgency — no clock-checking needed.

macOS Native

White noise that starts when you focus

DeadlineGhost controls macOS Background Sounds directly — no extra apps, no browser tabs playing rain loops. Real system-level white noise that activates automatically when you start a focus session.

  • Auto mode: sounds ON during focus, OFF during breaks
  • Works with all macOS Background Sounds (rain, stream, ocean, and more)
  • Three modes: Auto, Always On, Always Off
  • Native system integration — not a workaround

Urgency System

Four states of haunting

The ghost is a visual proxy for time pressure. You don't read a number — you feel the urgency.

Calm
0–25% elapsed. Gentle floating, long bar, soothing colors.
Approaching
25–50% elapsed. Moves closer, bar shrinks, slight agitation.
Hovering
50–75% elapsed. Breathing down your neck, rapid motion.
Enraged
75–100% elapsed. Shaking, red tint, bar nearly gone.
