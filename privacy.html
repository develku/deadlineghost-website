Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

The short version

DeadlineGhost does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your Mac.

Data storage

All your data — tasks, timer sessions, settings, skin preferences, and statistics — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. None of this data ever leaves your Mac. There are no user accounts, no cloud sync, and no server-side storage.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Usage analytics or behavioural data
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Crash reports (unless you opt in via Apple's built-in crash reporting)

Purchases

DeadlineGhost Pro subscriptions and purchases are handled entirely by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store. We do not process payments directly and do not have access to your payment information, Apple ID, or billing details. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for how Apple handles purchase data.

Third-party services

DeadlineGhost does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app makes no network requests beyond what Apple's StoreKit framework requires to verify purchases.

Children's privacy

DeadlineGhost does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and stores no personal data.

Your rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you uninstall DeadlineGhost, all locally stored data is removed with the app.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change our data practices (for example, by adding optional analytics or cloud sync), we will update this page and note the date of the change. Any future data collection would require your explicit consent.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at deadlineghost@icloud.com.
